Steve O'Hara

Licensed Professional Builder and Realtor

CSLB 1011871 & CA BRE License 01973292

Steve began his career in the construction and real estate industry with a creative design-build architectural firm in San Francisco. In the early 1980s Steve began selling and building homes for a development firm on New York's affluent Gold Coast. For 17 years Steve was a Vice President and Project Executive for two very successful land developers supervising the sale, construction, and the closing of hundreds of homes valued at over $350,000,000. He then formed his own construction company while a Licensed Real Estate Salesman in Westhampton Beach; specializing in land sales and managing building projects for investors, and representing the sale of many fine homes and properties in Southampton New York. In partnership with Tom Kelly, Steve's extensive construction and property development experience, his attention to detail and successful client service experience will create many opportunities evaluating property for family homes and investors portfolios. Steve is a Licensed General Contractor and a member of the National and California Associations of Realtors.
